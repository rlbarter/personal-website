This week I was asked to give a presentaiton on giving presentations.

So meta.

Anyway, my lack of desire to spend time writing something that had already been written by somoene else led me to a number of existing slides written by others with the same goal: to present on presenting. The only problem was that, although these slides gave excellent tips on making slides and subsequently presenting them, the slides themselves were perfect examples of what not to do when making slides. The slides were insurmountable walls of text.
